Inyoung Jang

My research interests are focused on the cultural phenomenon and its meaning formed by the interaction among communication/media technology, space, and human agency regarded as audience/citizen.
I have an M.A. in Communication from Seoul National University, and B.A. in Visual Communication Desgian & Mass Communication from Handong Global University.


I was born in Seoul, South Korea, and raised in Yantai, China. I like to spend my spare time traveling, hiking, and surfing the internet.
